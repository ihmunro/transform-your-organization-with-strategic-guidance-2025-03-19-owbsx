About Us

Founded in 2010, IDO Consulting emerged from a vision to transform how organizations approach strategic change. Our founders, with over 50 years of combined experience in organizational development and strategic management, recognized the need for a more holistic and data-driven approach to business transformation.

Our mission is to empower organizations to achieve sustainable success through strategic innovation and operational excellence. We believe in building lasting partnerships with our clients, delivering customized solutions that drive meaningful results, and maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professional excellence in everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical consulting engagement last?

Our engagements typically range from 3-12 months, depending on the scope and complexity of your needs. We work with you to establish clear milestones and deliverables throughout the process.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have extensive experience across manufacturing, technology, healthcare, and financial services. Our methodologies are adaptable to various sectors while maintaining industry-specific best practices.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear KPIs at the outset of each engagement and provide regular progress reports. Our success metrics typically include operational efficiency improvements, cost savings, and revenue growth.

What is your approach to change management?

We follow a comprehensive change management framework that includes stakeholder analysis, communication planning, training, and continuous feedback loops to ensure sustainable transformation.

How do you ensure knowledge transfer to our team?

Knowledge transfer is integrated throughout our engagement through structured training sessions, documentation, and hands-on workshops. We ensure your team is equipped to maintain and build upon the improvements we implement.
